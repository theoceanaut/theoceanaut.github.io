3

    The year is 2077. For the first time in the history of the earth, the end is coming because of only one specy: Homo Sapiens. This scenerio was expected for years but only to be ignored. The popular World “Green House Gases” is now too “popular” to ignore. There is no way to deal with them anymore because it doesn’t seem late but too late. The increase of CO2 in the atmosphere due to human activity causes the acidification of Oceans. The biological species in Oceans are extincting due to Ocean Acidification. The main agenda of the World is to deal with the Ocean Acidification because without solving this problem, it is impossible to deal with the climate change. However humanity needs a miracle to deal with this problem.
4

    Humanity is full of regrets. They are facing and trying to deal with the consequences of their ancestors’ thoughtless activities. They are getting out of hope. However, there is something that they don’t know: 66 years ago, 4 scientist working in NASA embarked upon to prevent the expected outcome of the Earth. They studied the different species and by bringing together their different traits, they created a new hibrit specy: OCEANAUT. Oceanaut, more legendery than the legend Mermaid, was put in to a time capsule to become a superhero when the right time comes. And now, in the year 2077, the time capsule was opened.
5

    WHOAT IS OCEANAUT? The bottom half of Oceanaut’s body is organic and the upper half is mechanic. While the bottom part has a fish body, the upper part is an astronout’s body. It can breath in CO2 into his body and then separate it into its components. It is a hibrit of organic and inorganic, technology and science. It is inspired by the nature. It is the first ocean astronout of NASA and is now ready for its first mission: diving into an ocean and decreasing the acidification of an ocean.
6

    Oceanaut opened its eyes under the ocean, took in its first breath: CO2. It gave out Oxygen and used Carbon for producing its own electricity. With every breath, it felt more energitic. Now, it was more ready to fight over the acidification. With its rich and unique algorithms, it was measuring the ratio of acid and optimizing itself according to the data that it stores. According to the saved data, it was adjusting the amount of the C02 that it takes in and O2 that it lets out. It was autonomous. It was conveying all the data to NASA continuously.
7

    Another quality of Oceanaut was that it could send signals to living beings under ocean to warn them about the places with the high level of acidification. So, one could say that it was both a ocean cleaner and poliçe. It was an Ocean Astronout.
8

    The first trial of Oceanaut was resonated worldwide. Noone knew the 4 scientists of NASA but the unity of 4 conscious brain prevented the end of the World. The first Ocean Astronout of NASA, Oceanaut, gave another chance to humanity one more time.
